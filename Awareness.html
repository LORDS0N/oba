    Awareness Page

    • Usability

      My blog is designed with usability in mind. I have implemented an intuitive navigation system with clear menus and a search bar, making it easy for users to find the content they need. The responsive design ensures that my blog looks great and functions well on all devices, from desktops to smartphones. I have organized the content using a clear layout with headings, subheadings, and bullet points to enhance readability. Additionally, I optimize images and use caching techniques to ensure fast load times. I regularly collect user feedback and conduct usability testing to make continuous improvements.


    • Accessibility

      I am committed to making our blog accessible to all users, including those with disabilities. My site supports keyboard navigation, allowing users to interact with all elements using their keyboard. I use semantic HTML and ARIA landmarks to ensure compatibility with screen readers, and all images include descriptive alt text. I have chosen high-contrast color schemes and scalable fonts to improve readability for users with visual impairments. My forms are designed with accessible labels and instructions, and I provide captions for videos and transcripts for audio content.



    • Inclusion

      My blog aims to be inclusive by catering to a diverse audience. I feature content from a variety of perspectives and cover topics that appeal to different groups. I offer translation options to make my content accessible to non-English speakers. I use inclusive images and stories that reflect the diversity of my readership. I encourage community engagement through comments and discussions, and my moderation policies ensure a respectful environment. I also review our content for cultural sensitivity to avoid stereotypes and offensive material.
